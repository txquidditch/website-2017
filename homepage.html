Tryouts are held for Texas Quidditch and Austin Quidditch at the beginning of every Fall semester and as needed in the Spring semster.

Teams

House Teams

Gryffindor, Slytherin, Ravenclaw, and Hufflepuff - the 'House Teams' - are our four intramural teams. They practice and play on campus. No tryouts are required and you are free to choose which House you want to play for!

Traveling Teams

Three-time world champion Texas Quidditch is our varsity team. Austin Quidditch is our JV team. Both of these teams compete against other Universities and Community (Adult) Teams. We play opponents such as Texas State, Texas Tech, Texas A&M, and Baylor. You must try out to play for one of these teams, but all members are invited to come and cheer for us at tournaments!
