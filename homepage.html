TEXAS & AQ


You may know TXQ as a three-time world champion quidditch team, and you could also help us light the UT Tower for the 4th time! Austin Quidditch serves as a JV team for Texas, and won the SW D2 Championship in the 2015-2016 season.

Open tryouts are held for these teams every fall semester, and tryouts may also be held during the Spring semester in special cases. Contact the captains for more information.
